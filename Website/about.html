About Us

As third-year students pursuing Bachelor's degrees in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the esteemed Instituto Superior Técnico, we were tasked with developing an innovative project as part of our course evaluation. This assignment marked the beginning of an exciting journey fueled by the challenge of devising solutions to real-world problems through innovation.

We express our heartfelt gratitude to Professor Marcelino Santos for his invaluable guidance and expertise as our Scientific Advisor. Additionally, we extend our sincere appreciation to Francisco Simplício for his dedicated support and assistance as our Monitor. Their contributions have been instrumental in our journey, and we are deeply thankful for their unwavering commitment and encouragement.
